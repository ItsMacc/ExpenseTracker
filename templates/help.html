Getting Started

Welcome to the Expense Tracker app! This guide will help you get started with managing your expenses efficiently.

Follow these steps to make the most of the app:

  1. Sign in to your account using your email and password.
  2. If you dont have an account, just enter your email and password and jump right in.
  3. Explore the "Track Expenses" section to add expenses based on:
    1. Date
    2. Category
    3. Amount
    4. Description (optional)
  4. Check the "Analysis" page for insights into your spending habits with a pie chart.
  5. Feel free to edit or delete expenses using the "Edit" button.

Adding Expenses

Learn how to add expenses to keep track of your spending:

  1. Click on the "Add Expense" button.
  2. Enter the date, category, amount, and optional description for your expense.
  3. Click "Add" to save the expense to your records.
